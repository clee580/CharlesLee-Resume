My name is Charles Lee. I am currently a 3rd year student at UCR, majoring in Biology and minoring in Education. As I graduated high school, I reflected on the classes that were memorable for me and realized that I performed better in classes where I liked the teacher. This made me curious about the education industry and how to become a better educator.

Having been both a Teaching Assistant for an Elementary/Middle School Teacher and a Teacher/Tutor at multiple after school programs, I have gained rich experience in aptly teaching coursework and engaging with students. As a TA, I learned a variety of methods to teach students while helping them become eager to learn more. The hard work of a teacher behind the scenes has especially been ingrained into my mind.

Being a Teacher/Tutor for after school programs have mainly taught me how to control a class of students of many ages.I have been in charge of students from 1st grade all the way to high school. These experiences helped me learn how to keep students attention, engage kids in their work, and cater to a student's learning needs.
